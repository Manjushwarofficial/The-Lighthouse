The Lighthouse

3D Survival Adventure Game

You play as a stranded mechanic who must repair a broken lighthouse, survive mysterious threats at night, and signal for rescue — all while navigating a remote and eerie island. Developed in Unity with atmospheric survival gameplay.

Download Game

Game Overview

Daytime Exploration

Move around the island, collect materials, and work on restoring the lighthouse. Explore the mysterious terrain and gather resources needed for survival and repair.

Nighttime Survival

Hide in shaded areas to avoid being hunted by nocturnal monsters. Use light and shadow strategically to survive the dangerous nights on the island.

Map Navigation

Use a hand-drawn map to explore and mark important locations. Navigate the island efficiently while tracking key points of interest.

Radio Communication

Signal for help using a radio. If successful, a boat or helicopter will come to your rescue. Master the communication system to call for aid.

End Goal

Repair the lighthouse, stay alive, and escape the island.
